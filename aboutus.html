Who Are We?

Boozey offers a diverse range of premium liquors and cigarettes sourced from reputable producers. With a commitment to quality and responsibility, we offer a curated selection of premium spirits and artisanal cigarettes, sourced from sustainable and ethical producers. We also try to help people understand the risks of smoking and encourage them to use our products responsibly. We're all about making sure people enjoy themselves while being careful at the same time.

Know Us

Get to know us better through our story, our values, and our commitment to excellence

Mission

Our mission is to provide top-notch liquor and cigarettes while looking out for people. We want to offer great choices in drinks and smokes, all from places that do things ethically.

Vision

Our vision is to be a trusted source for liquor and cigarettes, known for our commitment to quality and responsibility. We aim to create a community where people can enjoy their favorite drinks and smokes.

Brand Logo

Main Logo

Our main logo is a symbol of our commitment to quality and responsibility in the liquor and cigarette business. The design is sleek and modern, reflecting our dedication to staying current in the industry while maintaining a sense of quality. The colors are chosen carefully to evoke feelings of trustworthiness and reliability.

Alternative Logo

Our alternative logo color palette offers a fresh take on our brand identity, providing versatility while maintaining our core values

Logo Iconography

The iconography is designed to be versatile and recognizable across various marketing materials, signage, and digital platforms, serving as a visual anchor for our brand in the marketplace.

Brand Colour

We choose colors that reflect our brand values and make people feel a certain way when they see them. Consistency is key, so we make sure to use the same colors across all our marketing materials, websites, and products. This helps people recognize us easily and remember our brand

Brand Font

We choose fonts that reflect our brand personality and values, whether that's modern and sleek, or classic and timeless. Consistency is key, so we stick to a select few fonts that represent our brand across all our materials. This helps to create a cohesive and recognizable brand identity
